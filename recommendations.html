Recommendations — By Genre

Fiction

The Alchemist

Paulo Coelho
A simple, poetic fable about following one’s dreams. Travel with Santiago as he learns to listen to the world and to his heart.

The Kite Runner

Khaled Hosseini
A moving story of friendship, guilt, and redemption set in Afghanistan — intimate, heartbreaking, and beautifully written.

The Goldfinch

Donna Tartt
A sweeping, character-driven novel about loss and survival that follows a young man through grief, art, and adulthood.

Romance

Pride and Prejudice

Jane Austen
Witty, romantic, and sharp — Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy’s dance of pride and first impressions is timeless and deeply satisfying.

It Ends With Us

Colleen Hoover
A raw and emotional novel about love, choices, and courage. Powerful, intimate, and impossible to forget.

Normal People

Sally Rooney
A modern study of intimacy and class: two people navigate love and the messy work of becoming themselves.

Mystery & Thriller

The Silent Patient

Alex Michaelides
A gripping psychological thriller where a woman’s silence hides a terrifying truth — twisty, atmospheric, and unreadable once started.

Gone Girl

Gillian Flynn
A dark, clever domestic thriller full of unreliable narrators and sharp social insight. Expect surprises and a fast, tense read.

Big Little Lies

Liane Moriarty
A clever, twist-laden novel about secrets and suburban lives—a mix of humor, darkness, and mystery.

Fantasy

The Hobbit

J.R.R. Tolkien
A cozy epic adventure that follows Bilbo Baggins into mountains, riddles, and dragons. Gentle worldbuilding and grand charm.

The Name of the Wind

Patrick Rothfuss
A lyrical, character-rich tale about a gifted young man telling his life story — dense, immersive, and beautifully written.

The Night Circus

Erin Morgenstern
A dreamlike tale of a magical circus and two rivals whose enchantment becomes irresistible. Lyrical and atmospheric.

Self-Help & Motivation

Atomic Habits

James Clear
Practical, evidence-based strategies to build better routines — small habits that lead to remarkable results over time.

Ikigai

Héctor García & Francesc Miralles
A gentle guide to finding purpose and joy in daily life — lessons inspired by long-lived communities in Japan.

The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F*ck

Mark Manson
A refreshingly blunt take on choosing what matters, embracing limits, and focusing on what truly improves life.

Classics & Historical

Sapiens

Yuval Noah Harari
A sweeping, accessible history of humankind that blends science, culture, and philosophy into clear, provocative narrative.

1984

George Orwell
A stark, prescient classic about surveillance, language, and political power—dark, urgent, and deeply readable.

The Odyssey

Homer
The epic Greek tale of Odysseus’s long journey home — adventure, cunning, and timeless themes of heroism.
